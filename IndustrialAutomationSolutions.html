INDUSTRIAL AUTOMATION SOLUTIONS

Envision specializes in BIW (Body-in-White) robotics simulation & our advanced simulation
solutions are designed to optimize and streamline the automation processes in the
automotive industry. Using advanced technologies and systems, mechanical engineers can
automate and optimize industrial processes, increase productivity, and improve
operational efficiency with industrial automation solutions. Industrial automation solutions
typically integrate hardware, software, and control systems to streamline operations and
reduce manual intervention.
Here are some of their key features:

In industrial automation, robots are often used to perform repetitive or complex tasks with
accuracy and efficiency. Advanced software tools are used to simulate & optimize the
robotic processes involved in BIW assembly, such as welding, handling & material
placement. Using state-of-the-art robotic simulation software, we virtually program and
validate robot trajectories and sequences. This allows us to verify the feasibility and
accuracy of the robot's movements, ensuring collision-free operations, optimizing cycle
times, and improving overall productivity. Virtual programming minimizes the need for
physical prototypes and enables quick iterations and adjustments.
