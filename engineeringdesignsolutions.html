ENGINEERING DESIGN SOLUTIONS
   




As part of engineering design services, a range of activities are involved in developing and
designing mechanical systems, components, and products that are innovative and efficient. Envision
specializes in BIW (Body-in-White) fixture design, providing high-quality solutions for the automotive
industry.


Our expertise in fixture design ensures precise and efficient assembly processes for vehicle body structures.
An engineer's engineering design services begin with conceptualization and ideation, during which he or
she works closely with clients to understand their needs, goals, and constraints. Considering factors such as
functionality, performance, aesthetics, safety, and cost, they brainstorm and generate design ideas. 

 


Utilizing cutting-edge software tools, we create accurate 3D models of BIW fixtures. These models allow us
to simulate and validate the assembly process, ensuring that the fixtures meet the desired
specifications and are capable of handling the anticipated loads and forces. Engineering design services
often involve simulation and analysis to assess the performance and behavior of the design under various
conditions. Stress, heat transfer, fluid dynamics, and structural integrity are simulated and analyzed using
specialized software. This helps identify potential issues, optimize the design, and ensure it meets the
desired specifications and standards.

IMPLEMENTATION

We provide comprehensive documentation, including detailed drawings, bill of materials (BOM), and assembly instructions, to facilitate
smooth integration of the fixtures into the manufacturing process. Envision is committed to delivering exceptional BIW fixture design
solutions that optimize production processes, improve product quality, and enhance overall operational efficiency.

Engineering design services aim to optimize a design for performance, cost, manufacturability, and other factors. Based on feedback,
testing results, and client requests, engineers iterately refine the design. In this way, the most effective and efficient design solution is
achieved.
