BUILDING INFORMATION MODELLING


            
BIM is a process of generating and managing building data during its complete Life cycle, from conceptual design  through operation of
 the building.It is an integrated Work flow that enables architects, engineers, and builders to explore a project digitally before it is built.

            BIM is a modern technology and associated set of  processes to produce, communicate, and analyze 'building
 models'. Digital representations of the building components that follow parametric rules, which can be
 manipulated in an intelligent Fashion.
